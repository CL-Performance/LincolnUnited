Lincoln United vs Indy Saints FC

By SAM LAUGHLIN        June 4th, 2017

After the first 90 minutes in Lincoln United’s history, it is 'so far, so good.' United beat Indy Saints by a score of 

1-0 Sunday afternoon in the club’s inaugural league match.


The match was a cautious one with few chances, but after 76 minutes, United scored the one and only goal of the match. The goal came from wonderful build-up play from the back before it landed to the feet of midfielder, Daniel Maganda. Maganda linked with forward, Connor McFadden, who hit a beautiful strike from 20 yards out.

United’s next matchup is away from home against local Opponents, Lincoln Soccer Club, next Saturday at 7 p.m.

United will depart the following week for Indiana where they will face Indy Saints again, this time in Indianapolis, on June 17. United will compete against South Bend FC the following day.

A hard-fought first win will surely help build confidence as United begin their four-game away stretch this weekend.

Although this is only the beginning of the season, Lincoln United have plenty of reason to be optimistic. A hard-fought first win will surely help build confidence as United begin their four-game away stretch.
