Collin Valdivia

Goalkeeper


Hometown

Previous Team

Collin Valdivia joins Lincoln United from Creighton University for the 2017 season. Valdivia is a native of California where he played for CalSouth Premier League champions, FC Golden State, and Loyola High School. Valdivia will use the LUFC season to develop and gain invaluable experience as he prepares for the collegiate season in the fall.
