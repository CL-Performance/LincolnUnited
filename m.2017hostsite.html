Tuesday, March 14th, 2017 - Doane University Chosen as the Host Location for the 2017 Season


Lincoln United is pleased to announce that Doane University will be the official host site for their 2017 campaign. Doane’s campus is located just 20 miles south of the state capital, and boasts some of the best facilities in the state. The head coach of Lincoln United, Chris Lawson, had the following to say about the arrangement: “We are thrilled to have gained access to Doane’s facilities for our inaugural season. We are trying to build something special here in Nebraska that will provide the state's current high-level players with a chance to continue their development. Gaining access to state-of-the-art facilities is a huge step in achieving that goal.” 

The acquisition of these facilities adds another piece to the puzzle as Lincoln United look to chase league success in their first year of the CSL USA. Lincoln United is set to make their debut in Midwest Region of the league this summer, and as the season approaches, they look to be finishing up their preparation for it. Lawson had this to add, “These things are always a challenge and obviously a ton of work goes into a contract like this, but they [Doane] have been fantastic to work with. The moment I brought the idea to them, they really got behind it and have gone out of their way to make it an easier and simpler process for us,’ said Lawson ‘By going this route, it has freed up some extra time and resources that we have been able to pump into other aspects of the club.” While nothing is official yet, it looks like gym-access, housing & food options, player tracking technology and live-streaming are among the list of potential items that are in the works for the upcoming season.
