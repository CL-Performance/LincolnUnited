About Us

What is Lincoln United?

Lincoln United, based in Nebraska, is a semi-professional club that competes in the fourth tier of U.S. Soccer as a member of CSL’s Midwest Region. Lincoln United prides itself on being the top level of soccer available in the state with a combination of top-level collegiate players from nearby universities and other local talent. Lincoln United approaches the game with a passion for professionalism and player-development, and the club is already making waves in the Midwest during their inaugural campaign.

What does Lincoln United do?

Lincoln United provides a unique opportunity for high level players around the Nebraska area to continue their development in a professional environment. The club is devoted to the success of the players and strives to prepare them for the next level. Currently Lincoln United only offers this opportunity to male players, but the club has plans to make this opportunity available to everyone with a women’s side in 2018.
