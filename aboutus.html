About Us

Purpose Statement

Founded in 2017, Lincoln United Football was established as a value-added club in order to provide additional opportunities for high-level players during the summer months. Lincoln United approaches the game with a passion for professionalism and player-development, and as a semi-professional club, LUFC attempts to bridge the gap between amateur players and professional leagues by providing the most professional environment it can with top-level training, strength & conditioning, performance analysis and long term athletic development for every player. Despite being a part of this professional environment LUFC’s players will be able to keep their amateur status and continue to compete in collegiate or high school athletics.

Mission Statement

Lincoln United creates opportunities for top-level players, who currently reside or originate from the Greater Nebraska-Area, to continue their development by providing a high-level of competition and professional training environment on a day-to-day basis.

Core Values

Lincoln United was founded around a player first mentality, and the club strives to accomplish this by putting players before results, fans, and sponsors. We strongly believe in fostering the development of every one of our players, and while we compete at the highest level every day, we do not jeopardize player development to accomplish this. We also believe that feedback is at the heart of development, and as a club, we make every effort to keep open lines of communication between our players and staff.

Vision

Currently Lincoln United’s first team competes in CSL USA’s top tier, and the club has plans to introduce their U-21 team into CSL USA’s second tier in addition to the women’s side for the 2018 season. Going forward Lincoln United will continue to create more opportunities for players in the area with additional teams on both the men and women’s side, while seeking additional resources to help improve the environment for its current players.
