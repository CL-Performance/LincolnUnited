Coaching Staff Announced

Monday, March 13th, 2017 – Announced today by Lincoln United, Chris Lawson will lead the team and Erik Berkowitz will serve as an assistant during their 2017 campaign.

Head Coach - Chris Lawson
Lawson, who currently serves as the women’s assistant at Doane University, will bring a wide array of experience with him as Lincoln United prepare for their first season in the CSL USA. Lawson is originally a native of Virginia and has spent the majority of his playing and coaching career on that side of the country.

As a player, he competed for Bryan College (Tenn.) and for three separate NPSL Clubs: Chattanooga FC (Tenn.), Buxmont Torch FC (Penn.), and Virginia Beach City FC (Virg.). After his playing career, Lawson went on to Coach at Central Virginia United and served as the goalkeeper coach for the Buxmont Torch FC before accepting his current post and moving to Nebraska. Lawson currently holds an NSCAA National Diploma as well as goalkeeper licensing from the organization.

Lawson, a large advocate of the fourth tier of U.S. Soccer had the following to say: “They [semi-professional clubs] provide top players a great opportunity to play at a higher level while holding on to their collegiate eligibility. As a player and coach I have been fortunate enough to be able to be a part of a lot of professionally run organizations, and I look forward to being able to use what I’ve learned from those experiences here in order to give similar great opportunities to players and coaches in this area.” Lawson has already arranged two open trial dates, in order to give local talent exposure as a first step in proving these opportunities for them.

Assistant Coach - Erik Berkowitz
Berkowitz, who currently serves as the men’s assistant at Doane University, will bring a wide array of experience with him as Lincoln United prepare for their first season in the CSL USA.
