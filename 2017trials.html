Lincoln United Open Trials


Lincoln United will be hosting two open trials in April. Trials will take place on April 8th/9th, April 29th/30th and again on May 16th/17th in order to comply with NSAA regulations. The trials are open to all eligible and interested players. In order to compete in the CSL USA, players must be at least 16 years old and have obtained either U.S. citizenship or a valid visa by May 1st.

There is a $50 trial fee that will be put towards your player registration fee if you are selected. All trialists need to submit the $50 trial fee in addition to a release of liability before participation. All trials will be held at Lincoln United's host location for the 2017, Doane University. Directions to the field can be found below.

Registration for all three trials is now open, so feel free to register now or contact a member of our coaching staff for more information. In addition if you are unable to attend either one of our open trials conact us about remote trials.
